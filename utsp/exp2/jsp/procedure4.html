Procedure - Principal Component Analysis
 
These are the general guide lines and steps that should be followed to perform the experiment :
 
Step 1 : Select Image

Select the image on which you want to perform the experiment.
You can also upload your own images by clicking on "upload more images".
Step 2 : Select the method of PCT

You can select one of the method of smoothing from the available options. 

Step 3 : Click on the button "Process"

This will give you the Original bands images, Principal component images and eigen values and eigen vector values.
Step 4 : Enter number of bands required for inverse PCT

Here, the number of bands you will enter, will be the number of reconstructed bands from the principal components.
Step 5 : Click on "Process" button

This will give you the Inverse PCA in addition to Original bands images, Principal component images and eigen values and eigen vector values.
