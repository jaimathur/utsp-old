Procedure -  Texture Analysis
These are the general guide lines and steps that should be followed to perform the experiment :

Step 1 : Select image on which you want to perform Texture analysis
You can also upload your own images by clicking on "upload more images".

Note : Texture analysis only works on single band binary images.

Step 2 : Select direction to determine texture.
Step 3 : Select the feature for texture analysis
Step 4 : Enter value of displacement (>=1)
 
Step 5 : Specify size of convolving window.

keep this value in odd integers greater then or equal to 3.
Step 6 : Click on "Process" button

This will give you the desired textured image.
