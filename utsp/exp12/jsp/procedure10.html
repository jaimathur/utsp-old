Procedure -  Color Transform
 
These are the general guide lines and steps that should be followed to perform the experiment :
 
Step 1 : Select transform

Select whether you want to perform RGB to HSI or HSI to RGB transform.

Step 2 : Select image on which you want to perform the transform

You can also upload your own images by clicking on "upload more images".

Note: If you want to upload an HSI image then keep the name in the pattern like "HSV_anyname". While uploading HSV files keep the format ".tif" so it wont have any losses due to compressions in other formats.

Step 3 : If you have selected RGB to HSI transform then enter the color composite to be used for conversion.

Step 4 : Click on "Process" button.

This will give you the desired transformed image.
