Interpretation Of  Neural Network Based Classification Outputs

 

Above is a composite image taken from sample inputimage. It comprises infrared (band 4), red (band 3) and green (band 2). The landscape represented here is of a coastal area. On top of the image is the sea water. Major portion is vegetation, seen in dark red to bright red color and some patches of urban/built-up area (middle top) in light brown color.

For demonstration purpose, here, we have identified 5 different features in this image. Those are built-up area /concrete structures (bluish/bright), sea water, vegetation 1(dull red color), vegetation 2 (bright red color) and streets. We took the training samples of each class and used them for the classification of this image using back-propagation algorithm. (please refer to the procedure provided) .

CLASSIFIED IMAGE

Figure above is the image generated by the artificial neural network classifier. Here,

GREEN color area- represents SEA WATER

BROWN color area-represents VEGETATION 1 (dull red color in composite image)

MAGENTA color area- represents VEGETATION 2 (bright red color in the composite image)
